Private Deer Park Tour and Day Out in Leek


By Sprinks farm deer park


6 Reviews

Enjoy the rare chance to see British wild deer in their natural habitat and take photographs of them on this private guided walk around a private deer park near Leek. After an introductory briefing, stroll the park to spot its communities of red and fallow deer. Learn about deer behaviours, get photography tips, absorb the peaceful fields and Peak District views, and perhaps get the opportunity to feed the deer.


What to Expect What to Expect

Itinerary Great for a family day out, a valentines treat or team building. What we'll do: Deer park tour and photography experience. Have a chance to walk through our deer park and photograph the deer. We have Red and Fallow deer which can be frequently seen and photographs. This 1.5 hour tour will include an introduction to British wild deer and a deer park tour. Good waterproof shoes are recommended. The nature of the park is a space for wild animals and the tour is not suitable for people with impaired mobility or very young children.
